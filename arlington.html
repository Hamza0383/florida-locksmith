Serving Arlington's Mix of Homes and Businesses

One of the things that makes Arlington interesting from a locksmith's perspective is the sheer variety. You have longtime homeowners in brick ranch homes that were built in the 1960s, alongside apartment complexes with dozens of units, alongside small businesses and commercial properties that serve the neighborhood's daily needs.

Older homes often have lock hardware that hasn't been updated in years — sometimes that's fine, and sometimes it's a security risk worth addressing. We take the time to assess what you have and tell you honestly what makes sense for your situation. If your deadbolt is still solid and working, we'll say so. If it's worn and needs replacement, we'll show you why and give you a fair price on the fix.

For apartment residents and landlords in Arlington, rekeying is a service we perform frequently. Tenant turnover is a reality, and rekeying between tenants is the responsible, practical thing to do — it's faster than changing the locks and costs less, while giving new tenants full security in their unit.
