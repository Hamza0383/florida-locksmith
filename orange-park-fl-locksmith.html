Frequently Asked Questions — Orange Park Locksmith

Do you serve all of Clay County, not just Orange Park?

Yes. Our coverage in the Orange Park area extends throughout Clay County, including Oakleaf Plantation, Fleming Island, Middleburg, and Argyle Forest. If you're unsure whether your address falls within our service range, just call and we'll confirm quickly.

I just moved into a new construction home in Orange Park. Should I rekey?

Rekeying a new construction home is highly recommended. During the building process, keys are distributed to contractors, subcontractors, inspectors, and real estate agents. You have no way of knowing how many copies exist or who still has them. A fresh rekey immediately after closing gives you complete control over who can access your home — and it's typically very affordable.

Can you help with commercial properties along Blanding Boulevard?

Absolutely. The Blanding Blvd corridor is one of the busiest commercial zones in Clay County, and we service retail shops, offices, medical facilities, and other businesses along the corridor. Whether you need a master key system, high-security locks, or access control, we can assess your property and provide options that fit your business needs.

How long does a whole-house rekey take in Orange Park?

A standard home rekey typically takes between 30 minutes and an hour, depending on the number of locks and their current condition. We bring cylinder pins and keys for most common lock brands and can usually complete the entire job in a single visit. You'll leave with new keys that work on all rekeyed cylinders.

Are you available nights and weekends in Orange Park?

Yes. We take calls and dispatch technicians every day of the year, around the clock. Lock emergencies don't observe business hours, and neither do we. Call (904) 668-0776 any time you need us.
