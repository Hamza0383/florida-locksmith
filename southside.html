Frequently Asked Questions — Southside Locksmith

I'm locked out of my car at St. Johns Town Center. Can you help?

Yes — automotive lockouts near the Town Center and throughout the Southside area are calls we handle regularly. We'll dispatch to your location and get you back in your vehicle quickly. This service is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Do you work with property management companies in Southside?

We do, and we work with several in the area. If you manage a residential complex or commercial building in Southside and need a reliable locksmith for rekeying, lock repairs, and lockout response, we'd be glad to discuss how we can support your operation. Can you handle access control for a Southside office?

Yes. Access control is one of our commercial specialties, and it's in high demand in Southside's corporate and office park environment. Whether you need a simple keypad entry system or a more comprehensive electronic access solution across multiple doors and floors, we'll assess your needs and recommend an appropriate system.

I just bought a home in a newer Southside neighborhood. What should I do about the locks?

The first thing we recommend is rekeying. New construction homes typically have builder-grade locks, and the keys may have passed through multiple hands — contractors, inspectors, real estate agents, previous owners, and more. Rekeying changes the internal pins so that only your new keys work. It's fast, affordable, and the right first step for any new homeowner.

Do you offer smart lock installation in Southside homes?

Smart locks are very popular in Southside's newer developments, and we install them throughout the area. We can help you choose between keypad deadbolts, Wi-Fi connected smart locks, Bluetooth-enabled locks, and more. We'll match the right product to your door and your lifestyle and handle the full installation.
