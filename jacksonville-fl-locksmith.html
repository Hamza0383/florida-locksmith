Do you serve all of Jacksonville including the Beaches?

Yes. Jacksonville Beach, Atlantic Beach, and Neptune Beach are all within our service area. We regularly work in the Beaches communities and understand the particular needs of coastal properties, including hardware that holds up to the salt air environment near the Atlantic.

Can you rekey a home after I move in?

Absolutely. Rekeying is one of the most common requests we get from new Jacksonville homeowners. When you move into a new home — whether it's an older bungalow in Riverside or a new build in Oceanway — there's no way to know how many copies of the old keys exist.
