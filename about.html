Who We Are

About North Florida Locksmith

A locally owned locksmith business built on the belief that everyone in North Florida deserves fast, honest service — from their own neighbors.

Our Story

Built Right Here in North Florida

North Florida Locksmith started because we saw a real need in this community. Customers were calling national locksmith chains and either getting outrageous prices after a bait-and-switch, waiting hours for someone to show up, or dealing with technicians who had no idea where Mandarin ended and San Marco began.

We knew this area could be served better. So we built a team of skilled, professional technicians who actually live and work in Northeast Florida. People who know that Southside traffic on a Thursday afternoon is different from a Sunday morning in Riverside. People who care about the job because this is their community too.

From the beginning, our approach has been simple: answer every call, show up when we say we will, do the job right, and charge a fair price with no surprises. That's it. That's the whole philosophy.

Our Mission

Honest Service, Every Time

We're not interested in growth for its own sake. We're interested in being the locksmith that people in Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Orange Park, and across North Florida trust enough to call again — and recommend to their neighbors.

That means we tell you the price before we start. We explain what we're doing and why. We don't recommend services you don't need. And if something's wrong that we can't fix, we tell you that too rather than charging you for a job half done.

Whether it's 2 in the afternoon or 2 in the morning, the level of service is the same. A lockout doesn't care what time it is, and neither do we.

Why We're Different

Local vs. National Chain

National locksmith call centers look good online. They have big budgets for ads and polished websites. But when you call, you're not talking to a locksmith — you're talking to a dispatcher a thousand miles away who subcontracts the job to whoever picks up.

With North Florida Locksmith, you're calling us directly. We know the streets. We know the traffic patterns. We know which neighborhoods are harder to navigate and plan accordingly. And when we quote you a price on the phone, that's the price you pay.

  • Locally owned — not a franchise or national chain
  • Technicians who live and work in North Florida
  • Direct communication — no third-party dispatchers
  • Consistent pricing — no bait-and-switch
  • 24/7 availability with real, fast response times
  • Serving all of Jacksonville plus the surrounding region
