Emergency Locksmith Services We Provide

Whether it's 2 PM or 2 AM, these are the situations we handle every single day across North Florida.

🏠

House Lockouts

Locked out of your home? This is one of the most common calls we get — especially in residential neighborhoods like Mandarin, Riverside, and Arlington. We use professional tools to open your door without damage and get you back inside quickly. No broken frames, no drilled locks unless truly necessary.

🚗

Car Lockouts

Keys locked inside the vehicle — it happens to everyone eventually. We respond to car lockouts throughout Jacksonville and the surrounding area, working on cars, trucks, SUVs, and vans from virtually every manufacturer. We take extra care not to scratch or damage your vehicle during the process.

🏢

Office & Business Lockouts

When an employee loses a key, a lock malfunctions, or access is cut off before an important meeting, we respond fast. We serve commercial properties throughout the Jacksonville metro — from downtown office buildings to retail shops in Southside and beyond. See our full commercial locksmith services.

🔑

Broken Key Extraction

A key that snaps off inside a lock or ignition is more than an inconvenience — it can leave you completely stuck. We have specialized extraction tools to remove broken key fragments from door locks, padlocks, and vehicle ignitions without destroying the lock cylinder in the process.

🔧

Emergency Lock Repair

Sometimes a lock fails — the mechanism freezes up, the deadbolt won't retract, or a break-in attempt has left the hardware bent and non-functional. We carry replacement parts and can repair or replace the lock on the spot, restoring your security before we leave.

🔒

Post-Break-In Lock Replacement

If your home or business has been burglarized, the last thing you want is to spend the night with a compromised lock. We prioritize calls like these and can replace damaged locks immediately, often upgrading to stronger hardware at the same time. Consider pairing this with a full rekey or lock change.

What to Expect When You Call

A lot of people feel anxious during a lockout — especially at night or in an unfamiliar area. We try to make the experience as straightforward and stress-free as possible from the moment you call.

When you reach us, you'll speak with a real person — not a recording, not an answering service. We'll ask for your location, the type of lockout, and give you an honest estimate of how long it will take to get there. Then we dispatch a technician immediately.

When the technician arrives, they'll verify your identity (standard practice — we need to confirm you belong at the property or vehicle), then get to work. For most lockouts, we're in and done in under 30 minutes. Before any work begins, we'll tell you exactly what the job costs.

We don't surprise people with extra fees at the end of a job. The price we quote is the price you pay.

What to Have Ready When You Call

  • Your exact location or address
  • The type of lockout (home, car, business)
  • Year, make, and model if it's a vehicle
  • Any relevant details (apartment number, gate codes, etc.)
  • A callback number in case we need to reach you
