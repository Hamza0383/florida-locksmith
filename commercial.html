Commercial Locksmith Services We Provide

🔐

Master Key Systems

A master key system lets you control exactly who can open what — your maintenance staff might access common areas, managers can enter their departments, and a single master key opens everything. We design and install master key hierarchies for offices, apartment complexes, warehouses, and any property with multiple access points.

📺

Access Control Systems

Electronic access control takes key management to the next level. We install keypad, key fob, and card reader systems that let you manage access digitally — assign and revoke credentials without cutting new keys, log entry events, and set time-based restrictions. Great for businesses with rotating staff or sensitive areas that need close oversight.

🔒

High-Security Lock Installation

Standard commercial locks can be bypassed by a determined person with the right tools. High-security locks from manufacturers like Medeco, Mul-T-Lock, and ASSA Abloy use restricted keyways, hardened steel, and pick-resistant mechanisms to significantly raise the difficulty of unauthorized entry. We carry and install these systems for businesses that need a higher tier of physical security.

🚫

Panic Hardware & Exit Devices

Commercial buildings require proper exit hardware by fire code — push-bar crash bars, rim devices, and vertical rod exit devices allow safe egress in an emergency while maintaining security from the outside. We install, repair, and service panic hardware from Von Duprin, Corbin Russwin, and similar commercial-grade brands.

🔧

Commercial Rekeying

When an employee leaves — especially under difficult circumstances — rekeying the locks they had access to is a smart, immediate step. We can rekey entire suites or buildings quickly, and in most cases the existing hardware doesn't need to be replaced. We also handle rekeying when a business changes locations or takes over a space from a previous tenant.

📋

File Cabinet & Desk Lock Service

Lost the key to a file cabinet, storage room, or desk drawer? We open and rekey filing cabinets, lateral files, storage units, and office furniture locks. We carry a wide range of replacement cam locks and can often replace a lost-key situation on the same visit without needing to order parts.

🏢

Office & Business Lockouts

Getting locked out of your business is disruptive and sometimes costly — especially if employees are waiting or customers are already lined up. We respond to commercial lockouts throughout Jacksonville and surrounding areas as quickly as possible, with priority dispatch for businesses that are actively losing time. See our emergency page for more detail.

📱

Smart Locks for Business

Commercial-grade smart locks give you the convenience of keyless entry with audit trail capability and remote management. We install and configure smart lock systems appropriate for office environments, retail locations, and multi-tenant properties. Visit our smart lock installation page for more information.
