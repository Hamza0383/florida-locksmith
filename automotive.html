Automotive Locksmith Services

🚗

Car Lockouts

Keys locked inside? This is our most common automotive call. We use professional slim-jim tools and air wedge methods tailored to your vehicle type to get your door open without scratching the paint or damaging weather stripping. We're careful — your car's appearance matters to us as much as getting you back in quickly.

🔑

Ignition Repair & Replacement

If your key won't turn in the ignition, the ignition cylinder is worn, or you're having trouble with a sticky or binding ignition, we can diagnose and repair the issue on-site. In cases where the ignition cylinder needs full replacement, we carry common replacement cylinders and can often complete the job the same visit. We also handle situations where a key has been snapped off inside the ignition switch.

🔧

Broken Key Extraction

A snapped car key — whether in a door lock or ignition cylinder — needs specialized extraction tools to remove safely. Trying to dig it out yourself often makes the situation worse. We extract broken key fragments cleanly and then assess whether the lock or ignition cylinder was damaged in the process. In most cases we can cut you a new key right there on the spot.

📶

Transponder Key Programming

Modern vehicle keys contain a transponder chip that communicates with your car's immobilizer system. Without a properly programmed chip, the key will physically turn but the car won't start. We program transponder keys for most domestic and import vehicles using professional automotive programming equipment. This is often available at a fraction of dealer cost and done at your location. See our car key replacement page for full details.

📱

Key Fob Replacement & Programming

Lost a key fob or need a second one programmed? We program replacement key fobs for most vehicle makes and models. We can often handle this at a lower cost than the dealer, without requiring you to tow or drive the vehicle anywhere. Remote programming typically takes 15 to 30 minutes and is done at your home, office, or wherever the vehicle is located.

🚘

Trunk Lockouts

If your keys — and sometimes other valuables — are locked in the trunk, we can get it open. Whether the latch has failed, the key broke in the trunk lock, or the remote release isn't working, we have multiple approaches depending on the vehicle's design. This is a faster process than most people expect.
