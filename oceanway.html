Serving New Construction Homes in Oceanway

One of the most important things we do in Oceanway is help new homeowners secure properties after they've moved in. When you buy a new construction home, the builder has installed standard-grade lock hardware, and the keys to that hardware have likely been held by contractors, inspectors, real estate agents, and potentially others throughout the construction and sales process. You simply don't know how many copies exist.

Rekeying is the quick, affordable solution. It changes the internal pins of your existing locks so that only your new keys will work — without replacing the lock hardware itself. We can often rekey an entire home's worth of locks in under an hour, and it's one of the best investments a new homeowner can make in the first week of moving in.

For homeowners interested in going further, Oceanway's newer construction homes are often well-suited for smart lock installation. Standard door prep in new homes typically makes smart lock installation straightforward, and the results — keyless entry, remote access, and real-time notifications about who enters and exits — are genuinely useful for busy families.
