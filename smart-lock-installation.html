Why Go Smart

The Benefits of a Smart Lock

Smart locks have moved well beyond novelty. Today they're a practical security upgrade that gives you real control over who enters your home or business — without the hassle of managing physical keys.

Living in North Florida means a lot of time outdoors, frequent guests, and sometimes managing rental properties or vacation homes. Smart locks make all of that easier. You can let a contractor in while you're at work, check whether your door is locked from across town, and revoke access codes the moment someone no longer needs them.

  • No more fumbling for keys in the dark or rain
  • Create and share unique access codes for guests, cleaners, or contractors
  • Check lock status and lock/unlock remotely from your phone
  • Auto-lock features ensure the door never stays unlocked by accident
  • Activity logs show you exactly when the door was opened and by whom
  • Works with Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit on compatible models
  • Battery-powered — no wiring required for most installations

Our Process

How Smart Lock Installation Works

We make the upgrade simple — from choosing the right lock to walking you through the app.

1

Choose Your Lock

Call us and describe what you need. We'll recommend the right model based on your door type, smart home setup, and preferences. You can supply the lock or we can source one for you.

2

Professional Installation

Our technician arrives at your property and installs the smart lock correctly — fitting it to your door, aligning the latch, and ensuring it operates smoothly with no gaps or wobble.

3

App Setup & Configuration

We connect the lock to your smartphone, set up user codes, configure auto-lock settings, and test everything before we leave. You won't be left guessing how it works.

4

Full Walkthrough

Before we go, we walk you through everything — the app, the keypad codes, how to add or remove users, and what to do if the battery runs low. You'll feel confident from day one.
