Frequently Asked Questions — Mandarin Locksmith

How quickly can you reach Mandarin in an emergency?

Response times vary depending on traffic and where our nearest technician is located at the time of your call, but we aim to reach most Mandarin locations quickly. We serve the area regularly and keep response times as short as possible. Call us and we'll give you a realistic arrival estimate right away.

I just moved into a home off San Jose Blvd. Should I rekey the locks?

Absolutely — and this is something we recommend to every new homeowner. You have no way of knowing how many copies of the old keys exist or who might have them. Rekeying is quick, affordable, and gives you complete peace of mind that only your keys work on your locks. It's one of the smartest things you can do in the first week of moving in.

Do you work on older locks found in established Mandarin homes?

Yes. Mandarin has a lot of homes that were built decades ago, and the locks in those homes reflect that history. Whether you have an older knob lock, an original deadbolt, or hardware that hasn't been touched in years, we can assess it, service it, or recommend an appropriate upgrade without pushing you toward something you don't need.

Can you install smart locks at a Mandarin home?

Yes — smart lock installation is one of our most popular services in Mandarin's newer subdivisions. Whether you're looking for a simple keypad deadbolt or a fully connected Wi-Fi smart lock, we'll help you choose the right product for your door and install it properly so it works from day one.
